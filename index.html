Where's The Bus Stop?

In 2014, King County Metro, which provides bus service in the Seattle Metro area, proposed eliminating and shortening certain routes as a cost-saving measure. I was curious which parts of the city would have been most affected by the proposed changes, so I calcultaed the increase in time spent walking to the nearest bus stop.
